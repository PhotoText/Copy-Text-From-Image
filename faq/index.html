Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Copy Text From Image

Is it really free?

Yes. Copy Text From Image is free to use with all its features available to you at no cost. Whether you need to get text from image or translate it from picture, it's all free.

Do my images leave the device?

Never. All processing is handled locally. Copy Text From Image never transmits your pictures or recognised text to external servers.

Which image formats are supported?

PNG, JPEG, WEBP, BMP and virtually any screen content. Also supports clipboard images via copy-paste.

How do I use the extension?

Install the extension, then press Ctrl/⌘ + Shift + O anywhere to capture a region and copy the recognised text to your clipboard. Or click the extension icon and select the area you want to convert.

How do I get support?

Contact us at support@phototext.online
